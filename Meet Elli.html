Hi! I'm Elli! I'm a portrait photographer based in Northern Virginia and I'm a James Madison University Media Arts and Design alum. I come from a family of photographers and I take pride in delivering beautiful images. I take headshots, grad photos, couples and family portraits, and love doing themed shoots! (Cosplay anyone?) If you'd like me to take your photo, or you have an idea for a shoot you'd like to do, send me an email! When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me taking a walk or hiking in the Shenandoah, reading a book, or curled up watching a movie with a batch of homemade chocolate chip cookies.

Please send me a message!
